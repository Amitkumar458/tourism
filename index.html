Popular places to visit

Tajmahal

The Taj Mahal, the building immortalizing the love story of Shah Jahan and Mumtaz Begum is also one of the seven Wonders of the World. It has attracted tourists from all across the world. Taj Mahal, the epitome of a love is worth visiting, no matter how many times you have been there.

Qutb Minar

the Qutb Minar is the tallest masonry tower in India, measuring 72.5 metres high, with projecting balconies for calling all Muadhdhin to prayer. An iron pillar in the courtyard gave the mosque a unique Indian aesthetic.

Lakshadweep

“Lakshadweep, the group of 36 islands is known for its exotic and sun-kissed beaches and lush green landscape. The name Lakshadweep in Malayalam and Sanskrit means 'a hundred thousand islands'. ” India's smallest Union Territory Lakshadweep is an archipelago consisting of 36 islands with an area of 32 sq km.
